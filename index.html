Qui suis-je ?

My name is Alice Hagenbach and I am 200hr certified yoga teacher. I started practicing yoga in 2009 in Paris at a Bikram studio. Since then, I have moved to Strasbourg - with my 2 daughters and my husband, and have continued practicing hot yoga, as well as Ashtanga, Vinyasa, Iyengar and Hatha yoga…A variety of styles that have all influenced my personal practice. The classes I offer focus on developing strength, flexibility, balance and well-being. Proper alignment and breathing are at the heart of every practice. - classes in English – French - Italian

Yoga

Yoga means union. The physical practice of yoga aims to connect body and mind through concentration and breathing. Being fully present during your practice is foundational to yoga. The series of postures that are practiced during a yoga class can vary from being vigorous to restorative depending on the student, his age, his mood, his energy level.
